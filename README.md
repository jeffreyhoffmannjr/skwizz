# 🌟 skwizz

## ❓ What is skwizz?

**skwizz** is a secure, temporary image-sharing platform designed with privacy in mind. Upload your images and share them knowing they'll be automatically deleted after 24 hours.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🕒 **24-hour automatic deletion** at 12:00 AM CT.
- 📊 A **loading bar** at the top indicates the time remaining until midnight.
- 🔒 **Metadata removal** for enhanced privacy upon upload.
- 🛡️ **Filename masking** to obscure original file names.
- 🙅‍♂️ No account required for usage.
- 🖼️ Support for multiple image formats.

---

## 💡 Why Did You Develop This?

**skwizz** was developed as a thought experiment to test AI development capabilities and rekindle the simplicity of the early 2000s Internet:

- 🕰️ Nostalgia for a time when things didn't feel so permanent online.
- 🔍 A desire to create a platform emphasizing privacy and simplicity.

---

## ⚙️ Backend Technology

The backend of skwizz is custom-built in **Golang**, providing a secure and efficient infrastructure for handling image uploads and processing.
